Autopsy

Game Design Document
By Alvin Mei, Yu-Xiang Zheng, Andrew Ton

Introduction

This document describes Autopsy, a 2D side scrolling action platformer game. Different weapons and play styles will be unlockable as you progress through the levels and defeat enemies.

Backstory

You play as an undertaker and have to hunt souls that have escaped from hell using an arsenal of weapons.

(80 years ago, a hole thousands of kilometers in diameter opened up in the middle of the Pacific. No one knows why it appeared or where it leads to. Humans around the world have tried to investigate the formation and the interior of the giant window immediately after it appeared, but the efforts lead them to nowhere. Life must go on even with a giant opening on the planet's surface. At first the world seems as it was before. However, 5 days after the first opening, a second opening appears, and then a third. Humans quickly realized the world they’ve lived for generations has changed into something they don’t understand. Those giant openings are like a hole on a prison wall. It brought with it countless escaped prisoners from hell.

Through decades of struggle, humans have learned and are still learning the ways of fighting against those evil spirits. Although civilizations have vanished, terror and struggles remains, but a force of resistance has emerged, the Spirit Hunters. )

Objective

Finish the levels, defeat the bosses and reach the end of the game and bring salvation to the land.

Gameplay

The game will revolve around clearing levels of enemies.

The player can run, jump, and dash. The player also uses an arsenal of weapons to fight enemies with. The player starts with a simple long ranged weapon. Progressing through levels means the player gains more weapons, like a shorter ranged weapon for a tighter level. You will be able to move, jump, and dash. You will be able to switch weapons on the go depending on your needs.

Weapons

There will be four weapons.

  1. A short ranged scythe
  2. A long ranged pistol type weapon.
  3. An explosive grenade type weapon.
  4. An “ultimate” type weapon, think BFG from DOOM.

Enemies

There will be different types of enemies.

  • A simple fodder-type ghost only capable of contact damage.
  • A ranged ghost that shoots bullets in the direction of the player.
  • A bulkier, explosion resistant ghost that shoots more bullets at the player.

Levels

The levels will be fairly linear, the goal being to kill all the enemies and get to the end of the level. A mixture of linear and enemy wave based levels may be viable as well.

Levels 1, 2, 4, and 5 will introduce new weapons, in the order listed above. Levels 3 and 6 will feature bosses. Each level will also gradually introduce new enemies.

Controls

  • (Left Arrow), (Right Arrow): Move left and right respectively.
  • (Z): Jump.
  • (X): Attack.
  • (Shift): Alt attack
  • (Space): Dash.
  • (A, S, D, F): Switch weapons
  • (ESC): Pause game.
